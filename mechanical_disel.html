MECHANIC DIESEL

Course Overview

The Diesel Mechanic course is a one-year vocational training program that covers various aspects of diesel engines, including alignment and adjustment, addressing defects in vehicles, and maintaining diesel-powered equipment. The course is divided into two semesters and is offered at various ITI institutions across the country. Admission is available on a merit or competitive basis, and the course is designed to prepare students for various job roles in both commercial and public sector businesses

Course Contents

  • Introduction to Engine
  • Occupational Safety and Health
  • Electrical Safety Tips
  • Hand and Power Tools
  • Systems of Measurement
  • Fasteners, Gaskets
