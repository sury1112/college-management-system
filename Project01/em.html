Electronics Mechanic (EM)

Course Overview

ITI Electronic Mechanic is a 2-year vocational course where you will get to boost your expertise and intelligence with respect to electronic hardware. During the course program, you will be provided with theoretical as well as hands-on training for the fundamental concepts of electronic mechanics. This course expands your horizons for attaining favourable employment prospects in the consumer electronics industry, IT departments, commercial sectors, and many others. You can opt for various job roles such as Electronics Technician, Electrician, and Electronic Expert and have a prosperous career in the future. Moreover, the average salary that you can expect as a fresher in this sector may go up to INR 2 lakhs per annum. After gaining experience, you will see a significant increase in your salary.

Course Contents

  • Safety Precaution
  • Electrical Terms and Cables
  • AC and DC Current
  • Types of Meters
  • Cell and Battery
  • Semi-conductors
  • Ohm’s Law
  • Computer Networking
  • Transistor and Resistors
  • Soldering and De-soldering
  • Opto Electronic
