Fitter

Course Overview

An Fitter is a skilled technician trained in the mechanical fitting of machines and tools. This trade focuses on precision fitting, assembling, and disassembling parts, ensuring machinery and equipment function correctly. ITI Fitter courses are popular in India, providing essential skills for manufacturing, construction, and maintenance industries. The course lasts two years and includes practical and theoretical knowledge, hands-on training, and the use of various tools for fitting tasks. Fitters learn to interpret technical drawings, use hand and machine tools, perform maintenance and repairs, and ensure accurate alignment and functionality. They also learn to identify and rectify errors, troubleshoot issues, and find solutions to problems that arise during work. The trade offers numerous employment opportunities in various industries, including manufacturing, construction, service industries, and public sector organizations
